FLASH DONATIONS

Gifting that gives

Let’s face it. However much you want to invite all your donors to the fancy gala dinner, you probably can’t. The appetizers alone would blow your yearly budget. But that doesn’t mean every donor shouldn’t feel appreciated for all the good they’re helping do too.

That’s why we created Flash Donations. It’s gifting that gives. In exchange for a one-off donation, you can send your donor base customizable and exclusive offers, deals, discounts and experiences. Because one good turn deserves another. And 50% off pizza.
