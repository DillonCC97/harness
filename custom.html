CUSTOM DONATIONS

As simple as it sounds

When people have a little more, they want to give a little more. It’s human nature. So our Custom Donations allow donors to make a one-time secure donation, whenever and wherever it suits them. And as a thank you, your organisation can easily send custom donors a personalised message through the app to tell them about the impact they’ve made. It’s the lovely loop of giving.
